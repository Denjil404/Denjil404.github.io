About Me

I am a passionate game designer currently studying Game Design at IU International University of Applied Sciences. Actively working on projects in Unreal Engine and participating in game jams, I thrive on experimenting with new mechanics and refining gameplay experiences. My primary interests lie in roguelike progression, horror elements, and engaging upgrade systems, with a strong focus on delivering satisfying game feel. I am also interested in VR and have explored its potential in past projects, aiming to further integrate immersive design into my work.
