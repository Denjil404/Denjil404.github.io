Daniel Miller

Game Designer & Developer

I am a passionate game designer currently studying Game Design at IU International University of Applied Sciences.

Actively working on projects in Unreal Engine and participating in game jams, I thrive on experimenting with new mechanics and refining gameplay experiences.

My primary interests lie in roguelike progression, horror elements, and engaging upgrade systems.
